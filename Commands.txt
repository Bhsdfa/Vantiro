Terminal commands and syntax:
BH_TPS; 0..|..65535

EditMob[{%MobID%},{""}]
    ^ Changes a mobs data, the {""} field can contain:
    ^   Size, Speed, Health, Damage, BreakBlocks, MaxSpeed, DoKnockback, Smart, DoAI, Collision, Sprite, Class, ClassType, EntName, Group, AIState, TargetX, TargetY

SpawnMob; X, Y, Class, ClassType

SpawnItem; X, Y, Class, ClassType

TeleportEnt; %MobID%, X, Y

Teleport; X, Y

GetMobID[{$MobName$, %PutVar%}]
    ^ Will allow scripts to collect mobs IDs and store them in variables.

Noclip
    ^ TOGGLE.
Freecam
    ^ TOGGLE.

Stop
    ^ Stops server if multiplayer, closes client if singleplayer.
Cheats
    ^ TOGGLE: Enables/disables cheats in server.

Clear; 'Clears chat.

Reload; (Choice)
    >Configs
    >Map
    >

GameRules; (Choice), (Args per choice)
    >TileSize,
    >MaxWidth,
    >MaxHeight,
    >Tileset,
    >CalcLights, 0|1
    >ParticleLogic, 0|1
    >MobsLogic, 0|1
    >ItemLogic, 0|1
    >TriggerLogic, 0|1
    >DoMapScripts, 0|1
    >
    >

Render; (Choice)
    >MapLayers,
    >Mobs,
    >Particles,
    >Hands,
    >Lighting,
    >Items
