## Vantiro 1.1v07b
### Improvements from previous version (1.1v06b):

- New items system, now items can be tossed and picked up.
- Now supporting custom weapons, but WIP still.
- Removed old weapons, now can be summoned with the `"` key.
- Light is now at the Mouse.
- Light detail can be changed while running to High, Medium and low, keys `H`, `M` and `L` assigned respectfully
- Hands now have collisions with tiles.
- Removed Minimap, rewrite coming.
- Removed animations, rewrite coming.
- Under the hood changes.
