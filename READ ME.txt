## Vantiro 1.1v03b
### Improvements from previous version (1.1v02b):
- Better hud animation.
- Fixed the limits in the .INI doing nothing.
- Added a hammer to spawn zombies, zombies being: Normal, Slow, Fast, Biohazard, Brute and Fire.
- Extra hud to choose what zombie to spawn. (activated by pressing MOUSE3 with the hammer selected)
- Bumped the zombie and fire defaul limit. (able to be changed in the .INI)
- Added a bind to reload the .INI file on the fly, pressing the HOME key with debug mode on will BEEP and reload variables.
- Added more slots to hud, they're currently empty.
- Added a Sandbox check, if enabled the hammer and spawning will work, this comes enabled in the code.
